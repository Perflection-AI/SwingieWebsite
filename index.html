FEATURES

Unlock Your Athletic Potential with Real-Time AI Insights and Personalized Coaching at Your Fingertips.

Realtime Kinematics Data Insights

Leveraging deep learning vision model, our app captures and analyzes a golfer's motion data directly from camera feed. By tracking the golfer's body movements in real-time, our AI provides personalized feedback and actionable insights to help golfers improve their swing technique, accuracy, and overall performance.

Personalized AI+ Coaching

Our innovative training method combines the precision of AI with the expertise of real-person coaches to deliver a superior and personalized training experience. The AI analyzes performance data and identifies areas for improvement, providing instant feedback and tailored drills. In parallel, our seasoned human coaches offer personalized guidance, motivation, and strategic insights, ensuring a holistic approach to training.
