Vision/Mission

Our Mission

We want to help people fulfill unrealized well-being and achieve satisfying active lifestyles, making their experience in both learning a sport and bonding with other like-minded players 10X easier.

Our Vision

We aim to create an AI coaching copilot that understands human sports motions and provides quick and precise training advice to enhance sports learning experiences and worry-free progression for average sports learners.
